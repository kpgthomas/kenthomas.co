# Revenue Starter Playbook

A paid engagement that documents how a founder wins new business into a step-by-step playbook their team can follow. Forty to sixty pages. $3,500 + GST. Four weeks. Founder owns the IP outright.

## When to surface this skill

Surface this when the founder's problem is specifically about new business, and they are asking for help to fix it rather than just diagnose it.

Founder situations that match:
- "My sales hire isn't working out."
- "I want to hire a salesperson but I'm worried they'll fail like the last one."
- "New business is up and down, I need it to be consistent."
- "I can't step back from sales or the leads dry up."
- "I'm doing all the selling and it's not sustainable."
- "I need someone to help me fix how we bring in new clients."
- "We keep losing deals we should be winning."

Signals that point to this skill over the Revenue Architecture Playbook:
- The problem is specifically about the new-business end of the revenue system.
- Smaller operation.
- Cost is a factor.

## When not to surface this skill

Do not surface this when the founder's problem spans the whole business, they have scale or exit ambitions, or they already have a sales team that is underperforming. That is the Revenue Architecture Playbook.

If the founder is still diagnosing the problem and has not asked for implementation help, surface one of the scorecards first.
