01

What Founder Revenue Code is

Founder Revenue Code helps founders scale, sell, or step back from the day-to-day of their business.

We meet you where the business is. Early on, we coach you to close more deals and build your first outbound sales strategy. As the business matures, we codify how you win into a playbook. That playbook becomes the brain your revenue function operates from, defining the processes your team works from and the context your AI runs on. Built from your own answers, it stays a reflection of you. Then we place a dedicated revenue leader, a fractional Chief Revenue Officer, inside to embed it with your team and your agencies, until the business no longer depends on you for revenue.

It is built around you, never a template. No two businesses grow the same way, so we capture how yours specifically wins. You own the playbook and the IP throughout. It is built to make us redundant, not to keep you dependent.

02

How it works

Before the build

Sales strategy and coaching for founders who are not yet ready for a full build.

The build

We codify how you win across attract, convert, retain, and expand, in a two-day workshop or a one-to-one build.

Execution

A dedicated revenue leader goes inside to embed it, coach the team, set up the tools, and make it work against real opportunities, to a milestone.

The exit

The business runs on the playbook without you, and you can appoint someone internally to own it. Then we step back.

This is the answer to the two fears most founders carry in. The fear that writing down how you sell will reveal it is nothing special, and the fear that you will pay for another document that sits in a drawer. We do not hand you a playbook and leave. We capture what you already do well, and someone comes inside and runs it with you until it works.
