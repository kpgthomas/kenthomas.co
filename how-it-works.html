Common questions about the process

There are two Phase 1 options. The Revenue Starter Playbook is $3,500 + GST and takes 4 weeks. The Revenue Architecture Playbook is $10,000 + GST and takes 4 to 6 weeks. Phase 2 is $2,500 + GST/month plus an outcome payment, running for 6 to 24 months depending on how quickly your team achieves independence. Phase 3 is included at no additional cost.

The Revenue Starter Playbook focuses on your new business engine: how you attract the right opportunities, how you convert them, and what foundations need to be in place. It's for founders building their first repeatable sales process. The Revenue Architecture Playbook is comprehensive architecture that maps your entire revenue flywheel, covering everything from who you target through to how you retain and grow clients. It's for founders ready to build a team and remove themselves from day-to-day revenue generation. Some businesses start with the Starter and progress to the full Architecture Playbook later.

The Salesperson Preparedness Scorecard is a free diagnostic that evaluates how strong your new business engine is. It covers 15 questions across three areas: Foundation (your positioning, target audience, and infrastructure), Attract (lead generation and social proof), and Convert (your sales process from discovery through to proposals). It takes under 5 minutes and you'll receive a tailored report with recommendations mapped to the Revenue Starter Playbook. There is no obligation to proceed with KTCO after taking the scorecard.

The Revenue Independence Scorecard is a free diagnostic that evaluates your full revenue system. It covers 39 questions across four categories: Responsibility (who generates revenue), Foundations (your positioning and infrastructure), Methods (your sales and marketing processes), and Metrics (whether you're tracking the right things). It takes about 10 minutes and you'll receive a personalised report with recommendations mapped to the Revenue Architecture Playbook. There is no obligation to proceed with KTCO after taking the scorecard.
