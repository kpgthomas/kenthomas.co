Partner Ecosystem

No single firm can solve every revenue problem. That's by design.

KTCO operates within a partner ecosystem that extends the value delivered to founder-led businesses beyond what any one firm can provide. Every partner serves a specific function in the client journey.

01

How the ecosystem works

Most consultancies try to be everything. KTCO doesn't. If a founder needs something outside our scope, we refer them to someone who specialises in it, rather than trying to stretch our offering or hold onto revenue that doesn't belong to us.

Each partner in this ecosystem is here because we've worked with them, we trust them, and they deliver. Referrals go both ways: partners send businesses to KTCO when they need revenue architecture, and we send businesses to partners when they need something we don't provide.

The result is that no prospect who fits the broader ICP is turned away without a path forward. Whether a business is at the earliest stages of sales maturity or has outgrown what KTCO offers, there's always a next step.

02

Our partners

Foundational Sales Coaching

Founder Sales Solution

foundersalessolution.com

KTCO works hand in hand as a JV with Founder Sales Solution. It serves founder-led businesses that need foundational sales enablement before they're ready for a full revenue architecture engagement.

Founder Sales Solution provides entry-level coaching, sales training, and hands-on support to help founders develop their selling capability and build initial sales momentum. When businesses develop to the point where they need documented systems and scalable architecture, they're referred to KTCO for the Revenue Starter Playbook or full Revenue Architecture.

This ensures no prospect is turned away without a path forward. Businesses at the earliest stages of sales maturity have a structured entry point, and KTCO's architecture work is reserved for businesses with sufficient foundation to implement it successfully.

Best for: Early-stage founders developing their personal selling capability, businesses not yet ready for full revenue architecture.

GTM Technology Partner

SalesCommand

salescommand.ai

SalesCommand is KTCO's go-to-market joint venture partner. They provide the infrastructure, tooling, and execution support that powers KTCO's outbound campaigns, cold email at scale, and the technology platform behind our lead generation engine.

The partnership combines KTCO's methodology and market positioning with SalesCommand's technical capability in campaign execution, data management, and AI-powered sales tools. SalesCommand also contributes to the strategic development of KTCO's products and delivery infrastructure.

Best for: Businesses needing sales technology infrastructure, outbound campaign execution, and AI-powered sales tools.

Sales Recruitment & Enablement

Pointer

pointerstrategy.com.au

Pointer builds unstoppable revenue teams across Australia through talent acquisition, sales training, and enablement. When KTCO clients reach the point where they need to hire salespeople, Pointer is where we send them.

The relationship works because KTCO builds the system first, then Pointer helps find the people to execute it. This sequence is critical: most businesses hire salespeople before they have a documented process, and that's why 48% of first sales hires fail. Pointer and KTCO together solve both sides of the equation.

Best for: Businesses ready to hire sales talent, needing sales team training, or looking for embedded sales enablement.

Commercial Operating Architecture

CRO School

theb2bplaybook.com/cro-school

CRO School, built by Adem Manderovic and George Coudounaris around Closed Circuit Selling and Revenue Alignment Architecture, is KTCO's downstream partner for businesses with functioning revenue teams ready to take their commercial architecture to the next level.

When KTCO's fractional CRO engagement reaches the point where an internal resource is ready to take over, CRO School is where we send them. They learn the principles of closed-loop commercial architecture: aligning sales, marketing, and customer success so intelligence flows continuously, teams share truth-based signals, and growth becomes structural rather than heroic.

The progression is natural: KTCO builds the revenue system, embeds as fractional CRO to get it running, then hands over to an internal leader. CRO School equips that person to take the architecture further, turning a documented playbook into a self-sustaining commercial engine with predictable, compounding growth.

Best for: Businesses with functioning revenue teams where an internal leader is stepping into a commercial leadership role and needs to take the revenue architecture to the next level.
