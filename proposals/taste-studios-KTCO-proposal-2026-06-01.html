A focused start for Taste Studios

01

What we heard

A short read of where Taste Studios is sitting, drawn from our conversation.

Where you are

Around 1,000 active accounts, $3M revenue, a clear ambition to reach $10M over the next three to five years. Six years of operating history under Brand Your Own, with retention strong enough that accounts past their third order are typically sticky.

The live tension

Volume versus quality. The recent ads campaign brought more leads than the team could properly qualify, the engine got reactive, and the targeted-account work slowed. The right answer sits somewhere in the middle, but it has not yet been set.

What the team needs

The tactical sales layer has dropped off behind the mindset coaching. Three reps and a CSM with documented process and structured cadence are in place. The lift needed is targeted training, sharper qualifying, and pipeline discipline, not rebuild.

Succession in motion

Mel is the identified candidate to lead the broader team in six to twelve months. The growth needed is mindset and the ability to handle uncertainty, and that is workable.

02

Recommended start

Light, evidence-led, with the option to extend only when it earns the right.

The shape of it

Begin with a paid half-day audit of the existing sales function. Use the findings to focus a short run of ad hoc hourly advisory on the highest-impact tweaks. No retainer commitment, no embed, no fixed time on the calendar until the work proves itself.

If, after that, a tighter cadence or outcome-linked structure makes sense, we revisit it then. Not before.

03

  • HubSpot workflow review focused on rep usability and pipeline hygiene
  • Read of the commission structure and CSM cadence against current motion
  • Written report with prioritised recommendations and a SWOT against the current process
  • One follow-up call to walk you and Charmaine through the findings

Hourly advisory

$250 + GST / hour
  • Targeted sales training on the tactical layer, including question discipline and qualifying
  • One-on-one coaching with Mel to support the move toward team leadership
  • Working sessions on the volume-versus-quality balance and territory focus
  • HubSpot optimisation pass with your existing partner if needed
  • Ad hoc problem solving as issues surface, billed against actual hours used
  • Invoiced monthly in arrears, no minimum commitment
