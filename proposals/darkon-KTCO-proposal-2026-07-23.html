The one thing holding it back is that how Darkon wins revenue still lives in your head, and Paul's. Every important deal still needs one of you.

Our Solution

Over four to six weeks I work alongside you, Paul, Ryan, James and Joanne to get how Darkon wins work out of your heads and into a documented, repeatable system the team can run: how you attract, convert, retain and expand your clients. $30,000 plus GST for the build. There is no ongoing retainer and no outcome payment, because Ryan is capable of running the system once it is built. You own the playbook and the intellectual property throughout.

The raw materials of a strong revenue engine are all here. What is missing is not talent or market position, it is documentation. That is the most fixable kind of gap, and it is exactly what this engagement addresses.

Revenue Architecture Playbook (4 to 6 weeks)

What we'll do:

  • Attract: your ideal client defined and documented for each side of the business, your point of distinction (in-house Australian design and manufacture) captured as the anchor for every quote, proposal and campaign, and the demand approach documented so it connects cleanly to the marketing engine you run.
  • Convert: the sales process from first enquiry to won order, documented and repeatable, with your qualification criteria, your discovery questions, your objection handling and a consistent proposal and quote approach, so the team wins the same way every time and the deals that currently stall no longer need you.
  • Retain and expand: a deliberate process for referrals and repeat work from the specifiers, builders and clients you have already delivered for, which is your cheapest growth and currently untapped.
  • The measurement layer: the handful of core numbers to track so the pipeline is visible and the forecast rests on data.
  • CRM design: the specification for how your CRM should be set up to run all of the above, ready for implementation.
  • Operating rhythm: a sales meeting structure and cadence, aligned with the Scaling Up frameworks Jonathon Mond is already using with you, so this builds on that work rather than reinventing it.
  • Team enablement: the playbook handed over so Ryan and the team can run it, and the right salesperson profile defined so future hires succeed.

What you'll get:

A Revenue Architecture Playbook: the documented brain your revenue team runs on, covering how Darkon wins across attract, convert, retain and expand, plus a CRM design ready to implement. You own the playbook and the intellectual property throughout.
