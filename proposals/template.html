Phase Details

We'll build your revenue system in phases, proving each step works before moving to the next.

Phase 1

Extract: Revenue Architecture Playbook (4-6 weeks)

What we'll do:

  • Eight intensive sessions over 4 to 6 weeks: we extract everything about how you generate revenue, then you validate and refine what we've built until it reads like you wrote it yourself
  • Define your positioning, ICP, and competitive landscape so your team knows exactly who to go after, what to say, and why those [clients/customers] should choose you over your direct and indirect competitors
  • Map your complete sales process from first conversation to signed deal so every opportunity follows the same proven path, regardless of who is running it
  • Connect your marketing and lead generation directly to your sales conversations so the pipeline your team builds actually converts
  • Build your [client/customer/account] management framework so your delivery team knows exactly how to retain, expand, and generate referrals from every existing relationship
  • [Additional deliverable specific to their situation]

What you'll get:

A comprehensive 14-part Revenue Architecture Playbook. The most complete revenue document your business has ever had. You own the IP outright. If we never work together again, you still have a system your team can follow.

Phase 1

Extract: Revenue Starter Playbook (2-4 weeks)

What we'll do:

  • Define your positioning and ICP so your team knows exactly who to go after, what to say, and why those [clients/customers] should choose you over your main competitor
  • Map your sales process from first conversation to signed deal so every opportunity follows the same proven path
  • Build your outbound methodology: how your team generates new business conversations without waiting for inbound
  • Document objection handling scripts in your own language so your team can handle resistance the way you would
  • Create ready to use communication templates: proposals, email sequences, and follow-up cadences your team can start using immediately
  • [Additional deliverable specific to their situation]

What you'll get:

A focused 40-60 page Revenue Starter Playbook that documents how you win new business into a repeatable system your team can follow. You own the IP outright.
