Executive Summary

The Problem

The Sales Playbook v1 we built when we kicked off this engagement has been overtaken on three fronts: the ICP needs to widen into underserved verticals, the positioning needs to reframe away from labour arbitrage, and the outbound lead engine has matured under Mark. v1 no longer reflects where the business has moved.

Our Solution

A focused rebuild of the three sections that have shifted, followed by a three-month transition that has Mark fully running the playbook by 31 August 2026. I step away cleanly, with the team independent and the methodology and pipeline terminology protected.

The Investment

$3,500 + GST one-time for the v2 rebuild, plus the existing $2,000 + GST monthly retainer for the three transition months (June, July, August). Total engagement: $9,500 + GST over three months.

The Outcome

A v2 playbook the team executes from, and Mark fully running the revenue engine independently by 31 August 2026. From that date the engagement concludes and I step away.

02

What I Will Deliver

A focused rebuild of the three sections that have shifted, followed by a structured three-month transition that hands the engine to Mark.

1

ICP and target sectors

Rebuild

Widen the sector list from the original four ICPs into a priority tier and a watch tier, drawing on your expansion email and Mark's plan. Hold a firm size and deal-value filter on top so the team chases larger, multi-seat opportunities rather than small price-led work.

2

Positioning and messaging

Rebuild

Reframe Profitmaster as the operational scale partner for Australian growth businesses, with embeddedness as the moat. Add the direct-hire category to the competitive landscape, write in the five counters to Hunt Street, and bring the trust signals into the messaging: ISO certification, the 30-day Philippines exit rule, CPA Australia, the Australia-Philippines Chamber.

3

Outbound lead-generation engine

Rebuild

Document the call-first multi-touch cadence (call, voicemail, LinkedIn, call, email, recycle), the connected-call KPI framework, lead resurfacing of the existing HubSpot database, and the channel mix including the Outsourcing Advisor program and the Rapid Response Group as named lead channels. Referral partners stay in.

4

Transition to Mark

June to end of August

From the moment v2 lands, Mark co-runs the engine with me, the weekly sales meetings, the outbound cadence, the pipeline coaching, the Delia and EJ check-ins. Each month I step back further. By 31 August 2026 Mark is fully running it and I step away. The team is independent, the engagement concludes, and Profitmaster keeps the v2 playbook and the embedded operating rhythm.

What stays the same

SPICED and the SAL triage to discovery to pitch to proposal pipeline are working and stay as they are. You asked us to maintain the terminology we have built around the playbook, and v2 protects this language rather than reopens it.
